What is HomeCon ThermoSecure?

The HomeCon learning Thermostat is the first thermostat to get ENERGY STAR certified. It learns what temperature you like and builds a schedule around yours to help save energy.

Since 2011, the ThermoSecure Thermostat has saved billions of kWh of energy in millions of homes worldwide.* And independent studies showed that it saved people an average of 10% to 12% on heating bills and 15% on cooling bills.

    ThermoSecure Features

    Music

    Entertainment, hands free. Use your voice to play music, listen to audiobooks or stream shows, films and more to your TV with Chromecast.

    Forecast

    How can we ensure you’ll feel comfortable before you walk out the door? With our new User Interface, you are now able to check the forecast so you can perfectly determine your outfit of the day. Enjoy our new feature from your phone, watch, tablet, or laptop, from anywhere, anytime!

    Security

    With our state of the art security system integrated into the ThermoSecure, Homecon makes sure to combine all your platforms into one easy, reliable user interface. Feel safe leaving your home while your family is home, with ThermoSecures new motion sensors. We detect movement, when you aren’t there to see it.

    Temperature

    The most important feature of the ThermoSecure, Temperature, is the easiest to navigate. We want you to feel complete control within seconds of the temperature of your home. From your tablet, phone, laptop, or watch, you will be able to easily access your ThermoSecure remotely. Always be a comfortable temperature, it’s only a swipe away.
